Rules of the Game

The game begins by all players rolling one die. The person with the highest number starts; in the case of ties, dice are re-rolled. Play then continues counter-clockwise. In order to enter the game, you must roll at least 500 points to enter.

Each turn players can roll the dice. The player can choose to keep any combination of scoring dice. If a player does not score on a roll, their turn ends and they do not score their points (no exceptions). If a player gets a full combo (meaning all 6 dice have scored), they may roll all six dice again, until they choose to stop or a they do not score on a roll.

Play continues until the someone reaches to 10,000 triggers the final round. The person with the most points at the end of the round wins!
